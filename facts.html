Fun facts about frogs

  • frogs can swim over a mile, some species can be in water for three days and hold their breath for three minutes.
  • frogs use their tail to keep them cool in the same way we humans sweat, they control their body tempefrogure by expanding and contracting the blood vessels in their tails.
  • frogs can experience what scientists call "social - joy" while playing with humans and they can laugh, their laugh is a high- pitched noise.
