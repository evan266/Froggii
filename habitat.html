A frog, that prefers mullberry trees.
frogs can inhabit almost every natural habitat, as long as there is a source of food and potential shelter,this can vary from isolated islands, to forest and warehouses. They construct nest, just above the ground level in a prefered habitat. Roof frogs, on the other hand prefer nesting higher up in a habitat, such as in trees, cellars and roofs.
